A photographic portfolio of work by Richard Ashton

Richard Ashton is a semi-professional photographer based in Nottingham, England.
He specialises in portrait style street photography with a particular emphasis on capturing movement in sport and art.
His portfolio includes a range of climbing (traditional & bouldering), ballet & modern dance shoots.
After a career as an Ex Army Serviceman, Richard has worked as a climbing and outdoor pursuits instructor for over 20 years.
